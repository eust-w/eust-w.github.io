1.  打开File>Preferences->settings
2.  找到workbench.colorCustomizations
3.  修改setting.json

```json
{
    "editor.renderLineHighlight": "all",
    "workbench.colorCustomizations":{
        "editor.selectionHighlightBackground": "#9805ec",
        "editor.lineHighlightBorder": "#ff0000",
}
```
